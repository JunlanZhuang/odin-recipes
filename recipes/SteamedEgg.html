Steamed Egg

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Water
  • Chicken Stock
  • 1 tsp Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Crack 3 eggs in a liquid measuring cup and note the volume.
  2. Add salt, and beat for at least 1 minute
  3. Measure out water and broth at the same volume as the eggs and add it to the bowl. Whisk in the sesame oil, and make sure everything's well combined
  4. Pour the egg mixture through a fine-meshed strainer into a shallow heatproof bowl
  5. Place the dish of eggs in the steamer, cover, and steam over high heat for 3 minutes
  6. After 3 minutes have elapsed, shut off the heat. Let the eggs sit for 14 minutes with the lid firmly covered
  7. Remove from the eggs from the steamer, sprinkle with scallions, and serve
