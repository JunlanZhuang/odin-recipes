One Pot Mac N Cheese

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of whole milk
  • 3 cups water
  • 16 ounces of elbow macarone
  • 4 ounces of cubed Velveeta
  • 2 cups of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven, combine milk, water and macaroni
  2. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Reduce heat and simmer until macaroni is tender
  3. Reduce heat to low
  4. Stir in cheeses until melted
  5. Add salt and pepper
